MBA@UNC Elective Course Format

IMPORTANT NOTE: MBA@UNC (online) courses are not formatted the same as EMBA courses because it is operated by a team external to your home program. This also means that registration timelines are much different, and there is no flexibility with the timelines. When registration closes it cannot be reopened for any reason, so please make sure you register per the communicated MBA@UNC timelines.

On a space-available basis, EMBA students may take MBA@UNC elective courses at no additional cost.

The MBA@UNC Program operates on a quarter system with courses comprised of asynchronous videos and synchronous in-person sessions online. Use the filters below to view online elective offerings and course descriptions in MyKE.

MBA@UNC Guidelines

Attendance
 Attendance is critical. Students are expected to attend all scheduled live synchronous class sessions each week for the full 90-minute sessions, as opposed to the standard 120-minute sessions for EMBA. This structure is designed to accommodate the inclusion of more asynchronous material. Additionally, online-only courses have smaller class sizes. We ask that you do not sign up for an elective if you know that you will be traveling, etc. during the scheduled sessions.
Auditing Preparation and participation are crucial with smaller class sizes; therefore, all courses must be taken for credit.  Auditing is not permitted.
Prerequisites
 You may not begin electives if you have an incomplete in any core course. Courses must be taken for credit and must be completed with a passing grade.
How to Register
 You will register for MBA@UNC electives in MyKE. Online students will have priority enrollment in MBA@UNC electives. Please note that these course options are available provided that there is space in the course and that you have completed the necessary core and prerequisite courses. A list of electives where there are seats available will be provided prior to enrollment.
Technology and Course Materials
 Digital Campus (2U) is the Learning Management System for all MBA@UNC courses. You will receive an email from the Student Success team once your Digital Campus account is ready. Course sites are published approximately 14 days prior to the first day of class. Digital coursepacks containing cases, articles, etc., will be posted there.
Accessing Digital Campus

MBA@UNC courses will not be visible on your Canvas dashboard. Instead, Digital Campus (2U) serves as a separate instance of Canvas and is the Learning Management System for all MBA@UNC online electives. Onboarding instructions for accessing your Digital Campus account is emailed by the online Student Success team approximately 3-4 weeks prior to the class start.

Find an access link in MyKe or bookmark https://digitalcampus.instructure.com/Your username should be your first_last@kenan-flagler.unc.edu If you cannot find your activation email, please visit https://digitalcampus.instructure.com/ and click “Forgot Password” 
Support Information for Digital Campus Only
 If you encounter any technical difficulties, Online Technical Support is ready to help. You will also find their contact information by clicking on the Support tab in the Digital Campus.- Live-Chat directly from the Digital Campus

Tuition and Additional Costs MBA@UNC electives are available to you at your normal tuition rate. You are responsible for purchasing required textbooks; however, course packs are provided for you as part of your tuition. Students who drop within 2 weeks of the class start are subject to fees including course material costs.
Grades
 Grades are due to the MBA@UNC Registrar 1 week after the course has ended and will be posted in ConnectCarolina. MBA@UNC does not provide individual grade reports. Please contact EMBARegistrar@unc.edu should  you need a grade report for employer documentation.
